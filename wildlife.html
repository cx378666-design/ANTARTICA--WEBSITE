Wildlife of Antarctica

Despite the extreme cold, Antarctica hosts an incredible array of wildlife â€” from penguins and whales to tiny krill that sustain the entire ecosystem.

Emperor Penguins

The tallest and heaviest of all penguins, Emperor Penguins are known for their majestic stance and deep diving abilities.

Weddell Seals

These resilient seals thrive beneath the Antarctic ice, capable of holding their breath for up to 80 minutes while diving.

Humpback Whales

These gentle giants migrate thousands of kilometers each year, feeding on krill and small fish in Antarctic waters.
