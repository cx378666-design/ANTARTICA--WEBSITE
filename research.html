Scientific Research in Antarctica

Scientists from around the world conduct research on climate change, glaciology, and space weather in Antarctica.

  • Ice Core Studies – understanding Earth's climate history.
  • Atmospheric Research – measuring ozone and carbon dioxide.
  • Microbial Life – studying organisms that thrive in extreme cold.
